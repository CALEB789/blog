Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Fame Watch. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website famewatch.ug. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Subscribe to our push notifications

The information we may collect includes:

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver push notifications about new articles and updates

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Each of these services has their own privacy policy and methods of data collection and use.

Push Notifications

We use OneSignal to deliver push notifications. When you opt-in to receive push notifications:

  • You will receive notifications about new posts and updates
  • You can manage your notification preferences through your browser settings
  • You can opt-out of notifications at any time

Data Security

We implement reasonable precautions and follow industry best practices to protect your personal information and ensure that it is not inappropriately lost, misused, accessed, disclosed, altered or destroyed.

Your Rights
